Chrome Extension

Focus Tab Guardian

Focus Tab Guardian is a productivity Chrome extension designed to help users manage their screen time and reduce distractions while browsing.

The extension tracks time spent on websites and can temporarily block access when limits are exceeded, helping users stay focused on their goals.

Advertising disclosure:
Focus Tab Guardian may display sponsored content or advertisements only inside the extension interface and only after a user action. No ads are injected into visited websites.
